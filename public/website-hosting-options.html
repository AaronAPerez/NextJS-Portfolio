Overview

Every website needs two essential components to go live and be accessible to your customers:

  • A domain — Your website address (e.g., yourcompany.com)
  • Hosting — The server where your website files are stored and served

Below are the two hosting options available through AP Designs, along with recommended providers if you prefer to manage hosting yourself.
