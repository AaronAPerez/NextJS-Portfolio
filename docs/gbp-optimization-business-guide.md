## Service Overview

### What is GBP Optimization?

Google Business Profile (GBP) Optimization is a local SEO service that helps businesses:
- Appear higher in Google Maps search results
- Show up in the "Local 3-Pack" for relevant searches
- Generate more phone calls, website visits, and direction requests
- Build trust through reviews and professional profile presentation

### Why Businesses Need This Service

- **97%** of consumers search online for local businesses
- **46%** of all Google searches have local intent
- **88%** of mobile searches for local businesses result in a call or visit within 24 hours
- Most business owners don't have time or expertise to optimize their GBP

### Our Competitive Advantage

1. **Integrated Dashboard** - Custom client management system
2. **Automated Reporting** - Professional monthly reports
3. **Comprehensive Approach** - Not just GBP, but citations, reviews, and content
4. **Transparent Tracking** - Clients can see keyword rankings and progress

### 2. Outbound Prospecting

#### Google Maps Prospecting
1. Search for businesses in target niche + city
2. Look for profiles with:
   - Few or no photos
   - Low review counts
   - Incomplete information
   - No recent posts
   - Generic descriptions
3. Note specific issues to mention in outreach
4. Find owner contact info (website, LinkedIn)

#### Cold Email Sequence

**Email 1: Initial Outreach**
```
Subject: Quick question about [Business Name]'s Google listing

Hi [Name],

I was searching for [service] in [city] and came across [Business Name].
I noticed a few things that might be hurting your visibility on Google Maps:

- [Specific issue 1]
- [Specific issue 2]
- [Specific issue 3]

These are quick fixes that could help you show up for more local searches.

Would you be open to a quick 10-minute call to discuss? No sales pitch -
I'll just walk you through what I found.

Best,
[Your name]
```

**Email 2: Follow-up (3 days later)**
```
Subject: Re: Quick question about [Business Name]'s Google listing

Hi [Name],

Just following up on my note about your Google Business Profile.

I put together a quick audit showing exactly what's affecting your
visibility: [link to audit or PDF]

Happy to walk through it whenever convenient.

Best,
[Your name]
```

**Email 3: Value-add (7 days later)**
```
Subject: Free resource for [Business Name]

Hi [Name],

I know you're busy, so I'll keep this short.

I wrote a quick guide on the top 5 things [industry] businesses
can do to get more calls from Google Maps. Thought it might be helpful:
[link]

If you ever want to chat about your listing, I'm here.

Best,
[Your name]
```

#### Cold Calling Script
```
"Hi, this is [Name] from AP Designs. I was actually searching for
```

### Phase 2: Sales & Qualification

#### Qualification Checklist
- [ ] Business has physical location or service area
- [ ] Business is in a competitive market (worth optimizing)
- [ ] Owner has decision-making authority
- [ ] Budget of $200-500/month available
- [ ] Willing to provide GBP access
- [ ] Realistic expectations (3-6 month timeline)

#### Discovery Call Agenda (15-20 minutes)
1. **Introduction** (2 min) - Build rapport
2. **Their situation** (5 min) - Current marketing, pain points
3. **Audit review** (5 min) - Walk through findings
4. **Solution overview** (3 min) - How you help
5. **Pricing/next steps** (5 min) - Present package, close

#### Handling Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | "What's a new customer worth to you? Most clients see ROI in 2-3 months." |
| "I can do it myself" | "You could, but it takes 5-10 hours/month. Is that the best use of your time?" |
| "I tried SEO before" | "Local SEO is different. What specifically was done before?" |
| "Need to think about it" | "Totally understand. What specific concerns can I address?" |

### Phase 3: Onboarding (Week 1)

#### Client Onboarding Checklist
**Admin Tool:** `/admin/gbp/clients/[id]` → Onboarding Tab

**Day 1-2: Information Gathering**
- [ ] Send intake questionnaire or use intake wizard
- [ ] Collect business information (NAP, hours, services)
- [ ] Gather target keywords
- [ ] Identify competitors
- [ ] Request platform access

**Day 3-4: Access Setup**
- [ ] Receive GBP manager/owner access
- [ ] Set up Google Analytics access (optional)
- [ ] Get Search Console access (optional)
- [ ] Obtain website CMS access (if needed)

**Day 5-7: Asset Collection**
- [ ] Receive logo (high-res, square)
- [ ] Receive cover photo
- [ ] Collect 10-20 business photos
- [ ] Geotag all photos with business location

### Phase 4: Initial Optimization (Month 1)

#### Week 1: Profile Foundation
- [ ] Verify/claim GBP if needed
- [ ] Set primary category (most important!)
- [ ] Add secondary categories (up to 9)
- [ ] Write keyword-optimized description (750 chars)
- [ ] Add all services with descriptions
- [ ] Configure business hours + special hours
- [ ] Set service area (if applicable)
- [ ] Add business attributes

#### Week 2: Visual Optimization
- [ ] Upload logo
- [ ] Set cover photo
- [ ] Upload all business photos (categorized)
- [ ] Add alt text/descriptions to photos
- [ ] Upload videos (if available)

#### Week 3: Citation Building
- [ ] Submit to Tier 1 directories (10):
  - Google Business Profile ✓
  - Yelp
  - Facebook Business
  - Apple Maps
  - Bing Places
  - Yahoo Local
  - Yellow Pages
  - BBB
  - Industry-specific (e.g., Angi, Healthgrades)
- [ ] Verify NAP consistency across all

#### Week 4: Content & Reviews
- [ ] Create and publish 4 GBP posts
- [ ] Seed Q&A section (5-10 FAQs)
- [ ] Set up review generation process
- [ ] Create review response templates
- [ ] Respond to any existing reviews

### Phase 5: Ongoing Monthly Service

#### Weekly Tasks
| Day | Task |
|-----|------|
| Monday | Check for new reviews, respond within 24hrs |
| Tuesday | Publish weekly GBP post |
| Wednesday | Monitor rankings, note changes |
| Thursday | Check for profile issues/suspensions |
| Friday | Update client notes, plan next week |

#### Monthly Tasks
- [ ] Track keyword rankings (record in dashboard)
- [ ] Add new photos (if client provides)
- [ ] Publish 4 GBP posts
- [ ] Respond to all new reviews
- [ ] Monitor citation health
- [ ] Generate and send monthly report
- [ ] Client check-in call (quarterly minimum)

### Phase 6: Reporting

#### Monthly Report Contents
**Generate via:** `/admin/gbp/reports`

1. **Executive Summary** - Key wins and metrics
2. **Profile Views** - Search vs Maps, MoM change
3. **Customer Actions** - Calls, clicks, directions
4. **Review Performance** - New reviews, average rating
5. **Keyword Rankings** - Position changes, local pack status
6. **Content Activity** - Posts published, engagement
7. **Recommendations** - Next month focus areas

#### Delivery Schedule
- **Report generation:** 1st-3rd of month (for previous month)
- **Client delivery:** By 5th of month
- **Follow-up call:** Within 1 week of delivery
